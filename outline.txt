X) Effects
	a) Charm
		1) No effect on players
		2) Mobs and NPCs will attack anything that is attacking caster
	b) Fear
		1) players move randomly away from caster
		2) mobs path away from players
	c) Imprison
		1) mobs and player can't move at all
	d) Light Blindness
	e) Confusion
		1) player direction keys scrambled
		2) mobs loose target and wander
	f) Distortion
		1) Changes players view
		2) Mobs change travel direction
	g) Resistance
		1) Helps player resist negative magic effects
	h) Undeath
		1) Players stay "alive" until -20 hit points
		2) changes mobs into undead version
